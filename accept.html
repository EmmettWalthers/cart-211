You allow a lot more than you think

Accepting terms and conditions without reading them can be surprisingly dangerous. Hidden inside long, boring legal text, companies can slip in permissions that give them access to your data, your behavior, and sometimes even your identity. With a single click, you might be agreeing to data tracking, invasive analytics, unwanted charges, or the sharing of your personal information with third parties. Most users don’t realize how much power they hand over, often until it’s too late. Your consent is valuable, and giving it away blindly can have real consequences.
